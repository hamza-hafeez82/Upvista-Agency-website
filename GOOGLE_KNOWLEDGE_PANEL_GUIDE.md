## What is a Google Knowledge Panel?

A **Google Knowledge Panel** is the rich information box that appears on the right side of Google search results, showing comprehensive information about a company, person, or entity. It includes:

- **Company logo and images**
- **Description and founding date**
- **Headquarters location**
- **Founder/CEO information**
- **Social media profiles**
- **Number of employees**
- **Contact information**
- **Reviews and ratings**
- **Frequently asked questions**
- **Related searches**
